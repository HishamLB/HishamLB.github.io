Day Five

For the fifth and final day you will be training Abs and back again.

The workout:

  • Abs:
    • Crunches
    • The Plank
    • Hanging Leg Raises
  • Back

Crunches (4 sets of 15 reps)

Crunches are an ideal way to isolate ab training and it's a very easy excercise that could be done without any equipment.

How to perform Crunches
  1. Lie down on your back. Put your feet on the floor around hip-width apart. Make sure your knees are bent like in figure: ***
  2. Engage your abs while you lift your body upwards. Do not move your hips around too much.
  3. Return to starting position and repeat

The Plank(3 sets till failure)

One of the most popular excercise for good reason, the plank is a very intense excercise for beginners trying to build up ab muscles but it also could be scaled for seasoned trainers.

How to perform The Plank
  1. Start in the plank position, that is face down with your arms and toes on the floor. Your head should be relaxing and looking directly at the floor
  2. Engage your ab muscles and keep your torso straight and your body is rigid.
  3. Hold this position for as long as possible then repeat for the required number of sets.

Hanging Leg Raise(3 sets of 10 reps)

This excercise is pretty difficult but amazing for lower ab development.

  1. Start by hanging of a bar and raising your feet till they are at around hip height
  2. Hold this for a second then slowly return to the starting position and keep repeating this movement of crunches
