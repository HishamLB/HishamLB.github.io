Day two

For the 2nd day of the routine you will be doing the back and bicep muscles

Back:

The back is the biggest contributor to upper body physique and it is what gives dimension and strength that could prove useful in a lot of ways. The back consists of large muscles mainly the lats, rhomboids and trap(eziu)s. To train all of these muscles we will have to perform compound movements that utilize the back function efficiently.

The workout:

  • Back:
    • Wide Grip Pull Up
    • One Arm Dumbell Rows
  • Biceps:
    • Cable bicep curls
    • Barbell Curl
    • Inline Dumbell Curls

Wide Grip Pull Up (3 sets till failure)

The pullup is a really simply and effective excercise that utilizes your v-taper part of the back. Make sure you dont shrug your shoulders while doing the movement by pulling the shoulders "away" from the ears

How to perform a Wide Grip Pull Up
  1. Grasp a pullup bar with an underhand grip and fully extend your arm while bending your knees and keeping your core engaged.
  2. Pull yourself up till your arms are beyond 90 degrees and you can feel your back being raised
  3. slowly return to the starting position and repeat till failure.

One arm dumbbell row (3 sets of 12)

Rows are amazing for building back muscles as they utilize a great range of motion. For this excercise you are going to need a flat bench.

How to perform the One Arm Dumbell Row
  1. Select appropriate dumbell and lean forward and put your right hand on the bench
  2. Grab the dumbell and pull it towards your hip while keeping your elbow relatively close to your side
  3. Make sure your back is flat and abs tight then you can start the movement by pulling your elbow as high as you can and squeeze your shoulder blades together at the top. Lower the weight then repeat.

Biceps:

The biceps are arguably the most important muscles for the arm as they provide great strength when trained properly and compliment the upper body more physique wise.

Cable bicep curls (4 sets of 12)

Dumbbells are a great tool to train your biceps, but something that's often overlooked is the fact that at the top of the movement you lose tension in the biceps because of how gravity works and so a cable pull machine would easily solve that.

How to perform Cable Bicep Curls
  1. Adjust the weight at the cable machine and make sure it is lowered.
  2. Attach a horizontal metal grip and pick up the grip and start curling it slowly; be sure not to flare your elbows at all during the movement.

Barbell curls (3 sets of 15)

Another classic and pretty straightforward excercise that utilizes a barbell this time around, the barbell curl.

How to perform Barbell Curls
  1. Grab a barbell with a shoulder-width grip and let it sit infront of your thighs
  2. Brace your core and curl the bar up to your chest without moving your upper arm
  3. Return to the starting position and repeat.

Incline dumbbell curls (3 sets of 10)

Another curl?! Bicep excercise are motly just curls so you will be performing a lot of them here.lie down at a 45 degrees set bench and bend your elbows next to your body and start curling the dumbbells. Do this excercise for 3 sets of 10 reps.
